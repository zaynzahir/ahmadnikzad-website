Our Core Services

Comprehensive Import/Export Solutions Across Key Commodities

Oil Trading

Reliable supply of petroleum products and derivatives to global markets

Our oil division focuses on the reliable supply of petroleum products and derivatives to global markets. We work directly with trusted refineries and suppliers to secure consistent quality and competitive pricing. Our operations include the import and export of crude oil, refined fuels, lubricants, and other petroleum-based products.

We handle every stage of the process from contract negotiation and documentation to transportation and storage ensuring compliance with international regulations and environmental standards. By combining industry expertise with logistical efficiency, we help our clients secure their energy needs with confidence.

Cotton Trade

Premium-quality cotton sourced from leading producers worldwide

We connect textile manufacturers, distributors, and suppliers across continents with premium-quality cotton sourced from leading producers. Our cotton portfolio includes raw cotton, ginned cotton, and processed fibers suitable for a wide range of textile applications.

With a strong focus on quality control, we ensure that every shipment meets the specific requirements of our clients, from fiber length and strength to purity and packaging. By managing procurement, inspection, and shipping, we provide a seamless experience for buyers and sellers in the global cotton trade.

Dry Fruits Export

High-quality, naturally grown products from trusted farms

Our dry fruits division specializes in sourcing and exporting high-quality, naturally grown products from trusted farms. We work with a variety of dry fruits, including almonds, walnuts, pistachios, cashews, raisins, and dried apricots.

Each product is carefully inspected and processed to preserve its natural taste, nutritional value, and freshness. We prioritize sustainable sourcing practices and strict hygiene standards, ensuring that our customers receive products that meet both health and market standards.

Cement Supply

Premium-grade cement for construction projects of all sizes

We provide premium-grade cement to support construction projects of all sizes from residential buildings to large-scale infrastructure developments. Sourced from leading manufacturers, our cement meets international strength and durability standards.

Our logistics network ensures on-time delivery to ports and project sites, with the capacity to handle both bulk shipments and packaged cement. By maintaining consistent quality and competitive pricing, we help builders and developers keep their projects on schedule and within budget.

About Us

Founded in 2012, Ahmad Nikzad Khurasani has established itself as a leading global trading company dedicated to facilitating seamless import and export operations between Asia, Europe, and markets worldwide. Our journey began with a vision to bridge international markets and create opportunities for businesses across continents through reliable, efficient, and transparent trading solutions.

Over the past decade, we have built a reputation for excellence in the global commodities market, specializing in high-demand products that drive economies and industries. Our comprehensive approach encompasses the entire trade cycle from initial sourcing and procurement to rigorous quality assurance, efficient logistics management, and timely final delivery. This end-to-end service model ensures that our clients receive consistent, high-quality products while maintaining competitive pricing and meeting strict international standards.

Our success is built on the foundation of strong international partnerships, deep market expertise, and a robust supply chain network that spans multiple continents. We have cultivated relationships with trusted suppliers, manufacturers, and logistics providers worldwide, enabling us to offer our clients access to premium products and reliable delivery services. Our team of experienced professionals brings decades of combined knowledge in international trade, market analysis, and regulatory compliance, ensuring that every transaction meets the highest standards of quality, compliance, and timeliness.

As we continue to grow and expand our global footprint, we remain committed to our core values of integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction. We understand that in today's interconnected world, businesses need partners they can trust to navigate the complexities of international trade. That's why we invest heavily in technology, infrastructure, and human resources to provide our clients with the most efficient and reliable trading solutions available in the market.
