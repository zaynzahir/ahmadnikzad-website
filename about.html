Our Story

Founded with a vision to connect markets and facilitate seamless international trade, Ahmad Nikzad Khurasani has grown from a local trading company to a global force in commodity trading. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality products to markets worldwide while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics and customer service.

Today, we operate across multiple continents, serving clients in Asia, Europe, and beyond. Our success is built on strong relationships with trusted suppliers, rigorous quality control processes, and a deep understanding of global market dynamics.
